What is Qilaa?

Qilaa is a multidisciplinary company providing innovative solutions across multiple industries, including trade, IT, training, real estate, finance, legal consulting, humanitarian aid, and more.

What are Qilaa’s branches?

Our branches are located in Egypt, Jordan, Palestine, Indonesia, Turkey, and Bosnia, with our headquarters based in Qatar.

What industries does Qilaa operate in?

Qilaa operates in various fields, including: - Trade & Contracting - Information Technology & AI - Training & Consultancy - Real Estate Investment - Humanitarian Services - Legal, Administrative & Financial Services - Artistic Production - Banking & Finance - Strategic Partnerships & Agreements
