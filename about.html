About Qilaa

Qilaa Global Group is a multidisciplinary Qatari institution founded in 2012 by His Excellency Sheikh Dr. Abdulaziz bin Abdulrahman Al Thani. Since its inception, the group has become a symbol of excellence and innovation, committed to delivering added value to its clients through high-quality services that exceed expectations. From the very beginning, the group has placed customer satisfaction at the heart of its priorities, focusing on achieving the highest standards of excellence in all its services, believing in the importance of building sustainable partnerships based on trust and innovation.
